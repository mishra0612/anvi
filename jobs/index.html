FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

For Job Seekers

Need Help? Find Your Answers Here

Does ANVI charge candidates any fees for job placement?

Absolutely not. At ANVI, we strongly believe that seeking a job should never come at a cost. Our services for job seekers are 100% free. Our mission is to empower individuals and uplift communities—without charging a single penny.

How do I apply for job openings through ANVI?

You can browse and apply for openings on our website's "Job Openings" section. Once you find a suitable role, submit your resume. Our recruitment specialists will review it and reach out if your profile matches. You can also simply share your CV with basic details at contact@ANVI.in 

What industries or job roles does ANVI specialize in?

Over the years, we’ve built strong networks across all BPO, BFSI, Corporate sector. We place candidates at all levels—from entry to executive—based on your unique skills and goals.

How long does the hiring process take?

It varies by company and role. On average, it may take 1 week from application to offer. However, we keep you updated at every stage and guide you to prepare well for interviews.

Can ANVI help freshers or only experienced candidates?

We proudly support both! Many of our success stories began with first-time job seekers. Whether you're a fresher or an experienced professional, we work to match you with the right opportunity.

What if I’m not selected for a role?

Not being selected is never the end. We keep your profile active in our database and connect you to future opportunities. We also offer feedback to help you improve and grow professionally.

Can ANVI help improve my resume or interview skills?

Yes. We offer resume review, interview preparation, and career coaching to ensure you present your best self to potential employers. We’re here to help you succeed, not just get placed.

Is my personal information safe with ANVI?

100%. We follow strict data privacy protocols and only share your profile with employers after your consent. Your trust is our responsibility.

How often should I follow up after applying?

If you haven’t heard from us in 1 working day, feel free to follow up. We appreciate proactive communication and ensure transparency throughout your journey.

Why should I choose ANVI over other recruitment agencies?

Because we care beyond placement. At ANVI, you're not just a candidate—you’re a future builder. We work with integrity, empathy, and a deep commitment to helping you grow.

For Employers

Start Your Journey With Clarity

What makes ANVI Consultants different from other recruitment agencies?

At ANVI, we don’t just fill positions—we build partnerships. With deep industry knowledge and a people-first approach, we focus on quality over quantity, ensuring each candidate aligns with your culture, values, and vision. We view your success as our responsibility.

Which industries and roles do you specialize in?

We cater to a wide spectrum including all sectors. From entry-level to CXO roles, we tailor recruitment solutions to your business needs—no matter the scale.

What is your candidate screening process?

Every candidate undergoes a rigorous multi-step evaluation including skill assessment, background verification, cultural fit analysis, and detailed interviews. We present only those who are thoroughly vetted and aligned with your requirements.

How long does it take to fill a position?

On average, we deliver pre-screened profiles within 48–72 hours of receiving a requirement. The time to hire depends on the complexity of the role and your internal process, but we keep things fast, transparent, and efficient.

Can you support bulk or project-based hiring?

Absolutely. We have the capacity, experience, and tech-enabled systems to handle bulk hiring drives, project-specific recruitment, and seasonal workforce scaling, ensuring zero compromise on quality.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes. We stand by our placements. If a candidate doesn’t stay within the agreed clawback period, we provide a free replacement as per our service agreement.

How does your pricing model work?

We offer flexible pricing models—contingency, retainer, or project-based—based on your hiring volume, urgency, and role complexity. We ensure full transparency with no hidden costs.

Can you assist with confidential or executive-level hiring?

Yes. We handle executive and confidential searches with utmost discretion, tapping into our leadership network and conducting high-level candidate evaluations tailored to your strategic needs.

Do you provide value-added services beyond recruitment?

Indeed. We offer Staffing Services, Recruitment Process Outsourcing, Payroll Service & Franchise—helping you build and retain stronger teams.

Why should we trust ANVI with our hiring needs?

Because we combine heart and strategy. With over years of hiring expertise, we understand business impact, talent gaps, and market trends. Our focus is on long-term hiring success, not just short-term placements.
